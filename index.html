Welcome on P55B!
A unique place in the universe.

Build and manage your own bar

Your help is needed on the planet P55B. A new bar manager is wanted. That is your time to shine! Take controll over P55B and restore the bar to its former glory. React to the wishes of the customer and let them spread the word that P55B is back in the business. Soon your bar will be filled with customers from all around the universe. Will you be able to manage the crowd?

Design P55B to your own imagination

Buy new furniture and build a bar where the customers enjoy to hang out. If they leave your bar with a good feeling they'll probably bring their friends next time.

Collect ingredients

Managing a bar can be hard but don't forget to always make sure that you have enough ingredients for the drinks. Otherwise your customers might leave the bar and won't come back so soon. So keep an eye on the ingredient stock and check the space around P55B regularly for ingredients floating around. But be carful while collecting ingredients in the universe. There are also things floating around P55B that you should better not touch.

Make the customers happy

There are lots of different preferences out there in the universe. Make sure you're prepared for them by purchasing new recipes for the bar. If you have saved up enough money you can buy the fancy recipes that'll attract customers who are willing to pay a bit more for a drink. Also remember to update your bar machine when more and more customers enter your bar. Some of them might be impatient if they don't get their drinks fast enough.
